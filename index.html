ZERO (0) DOWN PAYMENT

While purchasing products and big-ticket items in particular, it might not always be viable for consumers to pay the entire amount upfront. While EMIs afford us the convenience of paying for the said item in bite-sized monthly instalments, quite often, we are asked to pay a certain amount upfront. However, by shopping at the Bajaj Mall, you can rest easy, and not worry about making any down payment. With the zero down payment facility at the Bajaj Mall, you can divide the total cost of the product into easy EMIs, which you can pay in flexible tenors as per your convenience.
